  - No system-level visibility of caseload risk, approaching deadlines, or capacity pressure                                                        
    - Current picture relies on asking individual caseworkers, not data                                                    
                                                                                                                                                      
    **For Applicants:**                  
    - Complete silence for weeks — no visibility into case progress                                                                                   
    - No way to get a meaningful update without calling a helpline
    - No confirmation that submitted evidence has been received or logged

    ## What We're Building

    A single unified service that:
    - Gives caseworkers **everything they need on one screen**: case summary, timeline, matched policy, workflow state, required actions, and deadline
     alerts
    - Gives team leaders a **dashboard view** of caseload health — flagging at-risk and overdue cases
    - Gives applicants a **self-service portal** to track their case status and see what's needed from them

    ## Why It Matters

    Caseworking is one of the largest categories of work in the civil service. Surfacing the right information at the right moment reduces processing
    times, improves consistency, and frees caseworkers to focus on the decisions that require genuine human judgement.
